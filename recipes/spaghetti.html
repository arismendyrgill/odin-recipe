Spaghetti Alfredo

Description

This creamy spaghetti Alfredo is a classic Italian-American comfort food. With just a few simple ingredients, you can have a rich and satisfying meal on the table in under 30 minutes. Perfect for busy weeknights or when you’re craving something cheesy and delicious!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Butter
  • Heavy Cream
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Parmesan Cheese (grated)
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Fresh Parsley (optional, for garnish)

How to make Spaghetti Alfredo

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, melt butter over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Pour in the heavy cream and bring to a gentle simmer. Cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add grated Parmesan cheese and stir until the cheese is melted and the sauce is smooth.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Add the cooked spaghetti to the sauce and toss to coat evenly.
  7. Serve immediately, garnished with fresh parsley if desired.
